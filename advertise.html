Massage & Bodywork we're more than just a magazine

For more than 35 years, ABMP has had a passion for excellence in the massage therapy and bodywork profession. In fact, we encourage our members to “expectmore” which is part of our mission. We love serving our more than 80,000 professional members and take great pride in producing award-winning content and thought-provoking stories.

But that's not all we do. We provide continuing education that can be found in our magazine, podcasts, blog articles, virtual online learning events, and education library. In fact, ABMP members have FREE access to more than 700 CEs with Certified level of membership in ABMP's CE Library!
