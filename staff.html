Editor in Chief

Darren Buford

Though not a native Coloradoan, Darren has lived in the Centennial State since 1996, when his life was transformed by seeing Boulder for the first time. Now a Denverite, he golfs, snowboards, bikes, hikes, and camps (as all are required by state law) in the 300-plus days of mountain sunshine. Born and raised in Kentucky, he's lived in each of the time zones in the continental United States, preferring the Pacific most of all. Darren studied English literature at Vanderbilt University (BA) and the University of Kentucky (MA), specializing in Romantic and Southern Literature, and still roots for the Wildcats, and now the Denver Nuggets and the Liverpool Reds, in his free time.

darren@abmp.com

  • Editor

    Jen Anderson

    Jen Anderson is the editor of ASCP Skin Deep and AHP Indie Stylist magazines. Jen has worked in media for the past decade, specializing in beauty and wellness. Before joining ASCP and AHP, she edited and wrote for companies such as Healthline Media, bareMinerals, and Refinery29. She holds bachelor's degrees in Journalism and Communication Arts from the University of Wisconsin-Madison and a graduate certificate in creative writing from the University of Denver. In her free time, Jen teaches ballet to teens and preteens.

    Mary Murphy began her editorial career as a reporter for The Gazette in Colorado Springs, Colorado, before moving to Denver where she worked stints at various print and digital magazines. An avid outdoorsperson, Mary enjoys climbing, hiking, backpacking, running, skiing, paddleboarding, and traveling. Closer to home, you'll find her practicing yoga, cooking, and spending quality time with her family, partner, and dog on Colorado's Front Range.
